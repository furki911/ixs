About Us

Based in Bangalore-India, Infinity Access Technologies(IAT) has been successfully delivering Systems Integration, IT Enabled Services and Consulting services to numerous clients. The company develops truly innovative and effective Management solutions based on a deep expertise in the leading network management and communication technologies. Our solutions utilize accepted standards and management frameworks.

The common objectives of IAT’s customers have been to achieve superior manageability of their products while maintaining project flexibility and avoiding lengthy implementation cycles. In all development engagements, our team has applied a standard of excellence that has turned every first-time client into a repeat customer.

IAT includes seasoned engineers with many years of experience in the design & development of complex ICT solutions. Having associations with fortune ICT companies & being connected with associations like ESC, STPI, NASSCOM enables us to offer endless IT Solutions with confidence & decency.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide exceptional service by delivering high quality and secure IT systems and solutions that allow our clients to effectively meet their business goals. We are committed to providing a challenging and rewarding work environment for our employees.

We are proud of our Company and the relationships we’ve built with our clients and the services we’ve provided to our community. Our work ethic and commitment to professional excellence are guided by our well-defined Core Values.

Enabling customers to accomplish their ambitions and realize their vision. By investing in our people, partners and technology we add value to those we serve with a focus on readiness, responsiveness and resolution.
